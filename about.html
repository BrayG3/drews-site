Meet Drew.

Your Guide

Drew G.

Drew is a certified USPA parachute guide based on the Gulf Coast of Alabama. He has completed a handful of professional skydives — and counting. What he lacks in sheer jump numbers he makes up for in genuine enthusiasm, a spotless safety record, and an unshakeable commitment to making your first freefall the best thing you have ever done.

An active creatine user, Drew stays in peak physical condition to ensure every tandem jump is controlled, smooth, and fun. He is also in recovery from a sports betting habit — low stakes, but still — which means he brings the same discipline and focus to the drop zone that he once reserved for Monday Night Football spreads.

No deaths. Ever. Zero mistakes in 2026 so far. For a man with a handful of professional jumps under his belt, that is a record worth celebrating.
