Common questions

Before You Jump

Do I need any experience?

None at all. Tandem skydiving means you are attached to Drew for the entire jump. Your only job is to enjoy it.

What is the success rate?

92% — and zero deaths, ever. Drew is transparent about this. Not every jump goes perfectly, but everyone has landed alive. That streak continues.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, close-fitting clothes and lace-up shoes. Avoid sandals, flip-flops, or anything loose that could come off at 120mph.

How far in advance should I book?

At least a week out is ideal. Drew is a busy man — or at least he tells people that.

Is Drew actually a good guide?

He is certified, he is fit, he is focused, and he has never lost anyone. For a man with a handful of professional jumps under his belt, that is a solid resume.
