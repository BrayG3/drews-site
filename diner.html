The atmosphere

Cold Beer.
Hot Grill.
Open Hangar.

Tucked inside Hangar 4 at the Gulf Coast Airfield, Drew's Bar & Grille is about as no-frills as it gets — and that is exactly the point. Fold-out tables, the smell of jet fuel and charcoal, and a front-row seat to watch jumpers come in for landing. There is no better place to eat a burger.

Open to jumpers, spectators, and anyone who wanders in off the tarmac. Cash and card accepted. Drew may or may not be cooking depending on whether there are jumps scheduled.
